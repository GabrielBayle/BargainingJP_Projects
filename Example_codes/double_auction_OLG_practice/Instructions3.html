    • While trading, you can select to act as a Buyer or a Seller by clicking on the corresponding button:

      • As a Buyer you can submit one bid at the time for a single share, provided that you have enough cash to pay if your bid is accepted.

      • Similarly, as a Seller, you can submit offer prices (ask prices) at which you are willing to sell each of the shares you own.

      • If a new bid is greater than an outstanding ask then a transaction occurs and the purchase price is the new bid price .

      • If a new ask is lower than an outstanding bid then the transaction occurs and the purchase price is the new ask price.

      • Buying a share reduces your cash balance by the purchase price, while selling a share increases your cash balance by the purchase price.

      • Note that neither your share nor cash inventories are allowed to fall below zero (no borrowing or short-selling).

      • Prices are determined only by the bids and asks submitted by the "active" investors in the market.

      • All outstanding bids and asks are automatically canceled at the end of a period.
