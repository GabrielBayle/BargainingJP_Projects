  • The assignment of roles (illustrated below) is as follows:

    • {# You'll receive an asset to sell and learn its intrinsic value, θ, ranging from L$0 to L$100, which is unknown to potential Asset Buyers in your group. #}
    • Before the first 3-Period Sequence:
      • Types are randomly assigned and participants are notifies privately about their own, either A, B or C.
    • At the beginning of each 3-Period Sequence:
      • Dividends to be paid per share at the end of the sequence are drawn randomly as follows:
        Probability Dividend
        1/3{{dividend_range_1|cu}}
        1/3{{dividend_range_2|cu}}

      • Five participants are randomly selected to be "old" members of Cohort Zero, endowed with {{shares}} shares each.
      • Five participants are randomly selected to be "young" members of Cohort One, and receive L${{money}} of cash.
      • The ten remaining participants stay "inactive" and partake in the prediction task until called upon.
      • Members of these two cohorts participate in trading in Period 1.

    {# What is known to everyone is that θ is equally likely to be any number between L$0 and L$100.#}
    • At the end of Period 1:
      • Five "inactive" participants are randomly selected to be "young" members of Cohort Two, and receive L${{money}} of cash.
      • The five "young" members of Cohort One become "old", retaining whatever shares and cash they hold.
      • The five "old" members of Cohort Zero exit the market, get to keep whatever cash they have (as part of their sequence payoff) and become "inactive" only participating in the prediction task until the next sequence.
      • When Cohort 0 exits, any unsold shares (worthless to them) will be distributed randomly to the entering cohort at no cost.
      • Members of Cohort One and Two participate in trading in Period 2.

    • At the end of Period 2:
      • The Five "inactive" participants that have not been "active" yet become "young" members of Cohort Three, and receive L${{money}}.
      • The five "young" members of Cohort Two become "old", retaining whatever shares and cash they hold.
      • The five "old" members of Cohort One exit the market, get to keep whatever cash they have (as part of their sequence payoff) and become "inactive" only participating in the prediction task until the next sequence.
      • When Cohort One exits, any unsold shares (worthless to them) will be distributed randomly to the entering cohort at no cost.
      • A "State of the world" A, B or C is randomly selected, with each state being equally likely. The selected state is communicated to everyone.

    • At the end of Period 3, which is the end of the 3-Period Sequence:
      • Dividends are paid to those holding shares only if they are of the same "type" as the "state of the world" regardless of whether they are "old" or "young".
      • Shares held by anyone with a "type" different from the "state of the world" will not pay dividends.
      • Shares do not pay any other dividends in earlier periods and are worthless after paying the dividend at the end of Period 3
      • Cohorts Two and Three exit the market and get to keep whatever cash they have plus any dividends.
