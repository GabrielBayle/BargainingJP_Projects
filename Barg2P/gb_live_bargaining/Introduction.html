Remark for the experiment

Thank you for participating in the experiment. In this experiment, we study people's decision-making in a controlled environment. Your participation is very important. Each decision you make impacts the results of the study.

Standard Rules:

  • Your decision-making will affect the reward you receive from the experiment.
  • Please focus on the experiment during its duration.
  • Follow the experimenter's instructions during the experiment and ask questions if anything is unclear.

Your compensation will be calculated based on your decisions and the decisions of other participants, and you will receive it in cash after the experiment ends, according to the rules described below.

To participate in the experiment, please read the following conditions and agree:

  • I agree not to talk to other participants during the experiment.
  • I agree not to use mobile phones, access the internet, or use any other forms of external communication during the experiment.
