Introduction to the Experiment

Welcome to our lab experiment. The purpose of this study is to understand decision-making processes in a controlled environment. Your participation is crucial, and your decisions will have direct consequences.

Standard Rules:

  • Your decisions are anonymous and will be kept confidential.
  • Each decision you make can impact your earnings from this experiment.
  • It is important that you remain focused and avoid distractions during the experiment.
  • Please follow the instructions carefully and ask questions if anything is unclear.

Your earnings will be calculated based on your decisions and those of other participants. At the end of the experiment, you will receive payment in accordance with the rules specified in the tasks.

We appreciate your participation and look forward to your contributions to this study.

Please read and agree to the following conditions to participate:

  • I consent to participate in this experiment.
  • I agree not to communicate with other participants during the experiment.
  • I agree not to use my phone, access the internet, or use any other form of external communication during the experiment.
