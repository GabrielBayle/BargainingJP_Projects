Bargaining Game Instructions

Welcome

Welcome to the Bargaining Experiment. In this game, you will be randomly paired with another participant to negotiate a division of a sum of money.

Objective

Your goal is to reach an agreement on how to divide {{ C.PIE_SIZE }} Experimental Currency Units (ECU) between the two of you.

Roles

You will be randomly assigned as either Player 1 or Player 2. The other participant will take the opposite role.

Making Offers

Enter your proposed amount in the input field and click "Send Offer" to propose a split of the ECU.

Responding to Offers

When you receive an offer from the other player, you can choose to accept it or ignore it and make a counteroffer.

Canceling Offers

If you wish to retract an offer before the other player responds, click "Cancel Offer".

Stopping Bargaining

If you prefer not to continue negotiating and accept the disagreement option, click "Stop Bargaining". This will end the game with a predefined disagreement payoff.

Time Limit

There is a time limit for the negotiation. If an agreement is not reached within this time, the game will end, and the disagreement payoff will be automatically allocated.

Payoffs

If an agreement is reached, the ECU will be split according to the agreed terms. In case of disagreement, both players receive a fixed disagreement payoff.

Communication

You can communicate with your partner using the chat feature during the game.
